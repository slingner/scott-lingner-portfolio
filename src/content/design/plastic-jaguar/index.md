Plastic Jaguar is a bi-coastal psych-pop and post-punk band with an emotionally dynamic, sonically adventurous sound. The visual identity needed to match — dark, moody, and a little unpredictable.

## Identity

The logo combines sharp geometric letterforms with an organic tension that mirrors the band's musical contrasts. The dark color palette with selective accent colors creates atmosphere without feeling heavy-handed.

## Deliverables

- Logo and wordmark (multiple lockup variations)
- Color palette and type system
- Web presence design and development
- Social media assets and templates
- Press kit layout
