Picture Book Review is a children's book review platform designed to help parents, teachers, and librarians discover age-appropriate books. The goal was a clean, welcoming interface that felt approachable for families while remaining easy to navigate and maintain.

## Design Goals

The site needed to feel warm and inviting without being cluttered — a challenge when the subject matter is inherently colorful and varied. I focused on a restrained color palette, generous whitespace, and strong typographic hierarchy to let the book covers do the visual heavy lifting.

## Features

- Browseable and searchable book catalog with age-range filtering
- Category and tag-based navigation
- Reader review submission system
- Responsive layout optimized for mobile browsing
- Admin-friendly content management
