A selection of logo and brand identity work for two distinct clients.

## Better Body Concepts

Brand identity for a personal fitness training business. The logo centers on a strong figure silhouette integrated with the company name — bold, clean, and immediately communicates the business's purpose. Delivered as a full brand package including business card design and marketing collateral.

**Skills:** Logo Design, Brand Identity, Business Card Design, Adobe Illustrator

## Josh Nelson Project

Logo design for a musician and band. The mark integrates a guitar silhouette with the initials "JNP" in distressed, vintage-inspired typography — giving the brand a rock aesthetic with genuine character rather than generic stock elements.

**Skills:** Logo Design, Music Branding, Typography, Adobe Illustrator
